The Dark Souls Series

The Dark Souls series is a series of action role-playing games developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The series began with the release of Demon's Souls for the PlayStation 3 in 2009, followed by Dark Souls for PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, and Microsoft Windows in 2011. The series is known for its challenging gameplay, intricate level design, and deep lore.

Games in the Series

  • Demon's Souls (2009)
  • Dark Souls (2011)
  • Dark Souls II (2014)
  • Dark Souls III (2016)
  • Elden Ring (2022)

Reception

The Dark Souls series has been well received by critics and players alike. The games are known for their challenging difficulty, deep lore, and intricate level design. The series has developed a dedicated fan base and has been influential in the action role-playing game genre.

Future of the Series

While the Dark Souls series has concluded with Dark Souls III, FromSoftware has continued to develop games in a similar style. Elden Ring, released in 2022, is a collaboration between FromSoftware and author George R. R. Martin. The game has been well received and is considered a spiritual successor to the Dark Souls series.
