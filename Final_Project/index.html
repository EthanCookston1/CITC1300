Welcome to the World of Elden Ring.

Elden Ring is the latest game from Fromsoftware, the creators of the Dark Souls series, and is a collaboration with George R.R. Martin, the author of the Game of Thrones series. The game is an open world game, a first for Fromsoftware, and has a lot of new mechanics and features that are not in the Dark Souls series.

Features of Elden Ring

Elden Ring has a lot of new features that are not in the Dark Souls series, such as a horse that you can ride around the world, a jump button, and a new magic system that is more like a traditional RPG. The game also has a day/night cycle, and weather effects that can change the world around you. The game is also known for just how big it is, with a world that is much bigger than any of the Dark Souls games.

Features and Items in Elden Ring

  • There are over 6 giant areas for you to discover in Elden Ring, with several caves, items, and bosses for you to find
  • Speaking of bosses, Elden Ring has a giant leap for Fromsoft, with there being at least 160 bosses for you to defeat.
  • To defeat that giant amount of bosses, Elden Ring has at least over 200 weapons, several different varations of magic and spells to use, and even creatures you can summon to help you out on your adventure throughout the Lands Between.
  • There are also several different endings to the game, with the game having a lot of replay value, and even a new game plus mode for you to play through the game again with all your items and levels.

Elden Ring's Reception

Elden Ring has been a massive success for Fromsoftware, with the game selling over 20 million copies in the first month of release. The game has been praised for its open world, the amount of content in the game, and the story that George R.R. Martin helped write. The game has also been praised for its difficulty, with many people saying that it is the hardest game that Fromsoftware has ever made.
