Dark Souls

Dark Souls is a video game series that has gotten wildly popular over the past few years because of series's creator Fromsoftware's latest big release Elden Ring, causing fans to go back to their older games and give them a try and eventually try to beat them all.

-The Chosen Undead, The Player Character of Dark Souls 1, wearing the Elite Knight Armor which is the poster armor of the game

Dark Souls Impact on Gaming as a Whole

Dark Souls is a series that has impacted video games in a giant way even with people not noticing it. Icons from the series such as the iconic Bonfire has shown up in several games like Doom,Fallout and Rainbow Six Siege.

Fun Facts about the Series

  • The Series started on the PS3 with "Demon's Souls",with a PS5 exclusive remake that came out in 2020 for anyone who wants to revisit the classic game.
  • With the game almost getting cancelled and being considered a failure at the time,but they decided to take a risk with the original Demon's Souls and eventually it would pay off.
  • Bosses from Dark Souls 1 , 2 , and 3 will be returning in Fromsoft's latest game, Elden Ring Nightreign soon.
  • The original Dark Souls Trilogy is completed and finished, but the gameplay and style still lives on with their current release Elden Ring
